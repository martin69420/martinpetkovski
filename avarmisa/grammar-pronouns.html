Avarmiša Grammar - Pronouns

Personal pronouns

Avarmiša distinguishes between exclusive and inclusive first person pronouns, as well as proximate and obviative third person pronouns. Additionally, Avarmiša features a reflexive and indefinite personal pronoun. These can be seen in the table below.
